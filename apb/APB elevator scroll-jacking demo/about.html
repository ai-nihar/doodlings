Who we are
Founded 1998

We started as a small team of engineers in Pune with one clear goal: build elevator components that don't just work — they last. For over two decades, APB (Advanced Precision Build) has grown quietly, earning trust one gearbox, one door operator, one safety system at a time.

In 2023 we transitioned to a registered LLP — not just a legal upgrade, but a reflection of how we work: collaborative, accountable, and long-term. Today we supply elevator manufacturers, modernisation contractors, and building owners across India and Southeast Asia. Our components move millions of people every day — safely, smoothly, silently.

We're still the same hands-on team, just better organised. No corporate fluff, no overpromises. Just precision engineering and real support when you need it.
