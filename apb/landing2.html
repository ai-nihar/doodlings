Upper Shaft · Wire Harness

Plug and play. Every wire, every floor.

APB's elevator wire harness systems are engineered for clean, reliable installation. Pre-assembled, tested, and ready to connect — the harness runs from the machine room to every landing and into the car, eliminating field wiring errors and reducing installation time significantly.

Car Level · Operation Panels

Inside the car. On every landing.

The COP (Car Operation Panel) board lives inside the elevator car — handling floor selection, display, door control, and emergency functions. At every floor landing, the LOP (Landing Operation Panel) board manages call buttons and indicators. Both are integrated into every APB controller package.

COP Board

In-car floor selection, 7-segment display, door control, alarm

LOP Board

Landing call buttons, floor indicator, wired via safety chain
